#####   Compiling to use Hadoop
javac -classpath <pathDoJarDoHadoop> -d <paraOndeVãoAsClassesCompiladas> <ClasseACompilar>
jar -cf <PathOndeQueremosQueFiqueOJar> -C <OndeEstãoAsClassesCompiladas> .

exemplo:
	javac -classpath ./hadoop-core-1.0.3.jar -d wordcount_classes WordCount.java
	jar -cf ./wordcountDDB.jar -C wordcount_classes/ .


#####   Put in Hadoop
hadoop fs -put <pathDoFicheiro> <pathDentroDoHadoopOndeColocarOFicheiro>


#####   Run in Hadoop
hadoop jar <pathDoJarEmLocalFS> <ClasseComMain> <PathFicheiroAProcessar> <OututFolder>
exemplo: 
	hadoop jar wordcount.jar org.myorg.WordCount /LoremIpsum.txt /output2
